Chart
=========
|icon-chart| Create various types of charts with customized functionality and appearance including historic charts with multiple y-axes, displacement charts and wind rose charts with configurable ranges.

To view custom charts navigate to :menuselection:`Chart View --> Custom`. You will see a list of all custom charts under the selected Node in the Workspaces Tree.
You can also display any pre-configured charts as a *Custom chart* tile on a :ref:`Dashboard <node-configuration-dashboard>`.

Types of charts available:

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 1

   historic/index
   windrose/index
   displacement/index
